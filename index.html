Musica X

MERU is an independent multidisciplinary artista who playfully swirls across genres and expression forms such as poetry, vocals, beat-making, playing acoustic instruments and making visual art. With a signature style, blending early Hip Hop, R&B, Neo-Jazz and Soul with her Afro-Indigenous roots and a message of unity. MERU aims to have a voice for the Motherland Venezuela with its current political and economic crisis, and a personal spiritual journey en la vida.
